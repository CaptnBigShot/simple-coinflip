Welcome to the support page for the Simply Coinflip app! Here's how to use the app:

How to Use

  1. Open the Simply Coinflip app on your Apple Watch.
  2. Tap on the screen to flip a coin.
  3. The result (heads or tails) will be displayed.
  4. Statistics for coin flips will be displayed.
  5. Swipe left or right to clear the statistics.
  6. Enjoy making decisions with the Simply Coinflip app!
